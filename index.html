About me

Hey there! I'm Adri, a 22-year-old Android Developer based in Barcelona. I've always been passionate about turning ideas into reality, which led me to mobile development—it's incredible how you can reach so many users right in their pockets!

I’ve worked as an Android Developer at Vueling, contributing to various projects, and I’ve also published my own apps. I love working on personal projects to explore new technologies and stay updated with the latest trends in mobile development.

While Android is my main focus, I see myself as a Mobile Developer, always interested in broadening my skills across all platforms. That's why I've been diving into Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) to build hybrid apps, ensuring my work is adaptable and future-proof. I'm also deeply invested in how users perceive and interact with the apps I create. This interest led me to earn a Google certification in UX design, ensuring that I don’t just build functional apps, but also ones that provide an enjoyable and intuitive user experience.

Outside of coding, you’ll find me climbing, hitting the gym, or even flying planes. If you want to connect or discuss any project ideas, feel free to reach out—I’d love to chat!
